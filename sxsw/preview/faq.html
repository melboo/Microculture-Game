faq.

What is the gameplay like?

Depends which game you play. The Metagame comes with six unique games. Some of them are about discussion and debate. Others reward careful strategy and planning. One is about knowing facts. Another is about making quick decisions.

How many people can play?

Most of the games work best with 2-6 players. Others can be played by many more. One of them - Massively Multiplayer Metagame - is designed for big parties and events.

Is this just another Cards Against Humanity clone?

Actually... Cards Against Humanity was inspired by an early version of The Metagame! Both games are part of a similar genre, along with older games like Apples to Apples, Fictionary, and Balderdash. But we think The Metagame is something very new.

Do you have plans for new expansion sets?

Yes! Look for new expansion packs coming in the future.
